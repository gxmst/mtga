## 简体中文

### 🛡️ 模组简介
**TACZ MTGA** 是一款致力于将 **Timeless and Classics Zero (TacZ)** 枪械系统与自动化防御完美结合的史诗级模组。在这片充满挑战的方块大陆上，单纯的手动射击已不足以抵御无尽的威胁。你需要更强大、更智能、更坚固的防御塔。

### 🌟 核心特性
*   **全线适配**：支持绝大多数 TACZ 现代枪械，化身全自动割草机。
*   **深度交互**：
    *   **挂载系统**：空手右键即可精准部署手中利刃。
    *   **快速部署**：Shift + 左键一键拆卸，随时随地机动转移。
*   **战术指挥**：支持红石信号远程停机，满足复杂的防区规划。
*   **移动堡垒**：可完美承载于矿车与船只之上，打造你的移动防御阵线。
*   **晋升之路**：独有的击杀计数系统。当炮台跨越 300 杀门槛时，将晋升为“老兵级”，获得金色荣耀标识与恐怖的战力加成。

### 🛠️ 模块化升级
*   **📡 火控雷达 (Range Upgrade)**：开启超远视距，侦测范围翻倍 (x2)。
*   **❄️ 水冷系统 (Speed Upgrade)**：极大优化散热，提升 20% 循环射速。
*   **🏭 弹药制造工厂 (Infinite Ammo)**：实现终极弹药自由。*平衡性：攻击力调整为原有的 15%*。

## English

### 🛡️ Introduction
**TACZ MTGA** is an epic expansion designed to seamlessly integrate the **Timeless and Classics Zero (TacZ)** weapon system with advanced automated defense. In a world where manual firepower isn't enough, we bring you the ultimate tactical solution.

### 🌟 Key Features
*   **Full Integration**: Compatible with almost all TACZ modern firearms.
*   **Tactical Interaction**:
    *   **Mounting**: Right-click with an empty hand to mount your weapon.
    *   **Dismantle**: Shift + Left-click to revert the turret to item form for rapid redeployment.
*   **Strategic Control**: Redstone compatibility allows for remote deactivation and complex area denial setups.
*   **Mobile Fortifications**: Turrets can be pushed into minecarts or boats, creating lethal mobile platforms.
*   **Veterancy System**: Every kill counts. Reaching 300 kills grants "Veteran" status, unlocking a golden nameplate and devastating power.

### 🛠️ Upgrades
*   **📡 Fire Control Radar (Range Upgrade)**: Enables extreme tracking, doubling the detection range (x2).
*   **❄️ Water Cooling System (Speed Upgrade)**: Enhances thermal management, increasing fire rate by 20%.
*   **🏭 Ammo Manufacturing Factory (Infinite Ammo)**: Achieving true logistic independence. *Balance: Damage is reduced to 15% of the base value*.
